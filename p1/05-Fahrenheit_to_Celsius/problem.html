Create a program that allows the user to enter high and low temperatures (measured in Fahrenheit degrees), then converts and displays the temperatures in Celsius degrees. Notice that the input and output are integers.

To convert a Fahrenheit temperature F to Celsius degrees, find (F-32)*(5/9)

Your program must utilize a function int FahrenheitToCelsius( int fahrenheit ). 

Enter high and low temperatures (Fahrenheit): 212 32

High (Celsius): 100
Low (Celsius): 0

Enter high and low temperatures (Fahrenheit): 80 60

High (Celsius): 26
Low (Celsius): 15
