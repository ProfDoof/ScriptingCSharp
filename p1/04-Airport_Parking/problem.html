If you have ever parked at an airport, you have probably noticed that airports typically have complicated rules for determining the cost of parking. Here is a sample pricing schedule, taken from a real airport.

Pricing

Time Cost
First 30 minutes FREE
31 to 60 minutes $2.00
Each additional 30 minutes $1.00
Up to seven hours $14.00
Thereafter, each hour $1.00
24-hour maximum $21.00

Create a program to input a time (in minutes) that a car has been parked and then display the cost of parking, according to the pricing scheduled shown above.

As noted above, this pricing is from a real airport and the presentation is unclear. Please note that "each additional 30 minutes" means each block of up to 30 minutes, so 65 minutes costs $3 (1 hour + 1 30-minute period). Each hour after the first seven adds $1 to the cost, but the total cost will not exceed $21 for any time within 24 hours. This program will not be tested with times exceeding 24 hours.
